Who Can Participate?

All Hack Clubbers globally under the age of 18 are eligible! Any US high school students over the age of 18 can join. This is your opportunity to flaunt your coding prowess and grab some astounding hardware prizes!

What Is BURGERS?

Best described, BURGERS (Build Ur Really Good Extraordinary Rad Site) is a Y.S.W.S(You Ship We Ship) event where you will develop a full-stack project with extensive multi-dimensional creativity and inventiveness.

Depending on your project's quality and complexity, you can earn burger grants from $10-$20 and Raspberry Pi hardware rewards which are unlocked per each patty.

Check the Requirements page for detailed specifications and the Rules page for the steps needed to be eligible.

How To Build Your Project

BURGERS affirms your ability to design a full-stack application with both frontend and backend components integrating your skills and imagination.

Incorporate advanced research and bold creative web development techniques to differentiate your project. Most importantly, write your own code and give credit to any other works that inspired your design.

Consult our extensive Get Started page for information, technology suggestions, and helpful advice to enhance the quality of your project.
